The Atelier

Mehndi as
haute couture.

Founded in 2014 by Zara El-Amin, our maison treats henna as an heirloom art form. Every bride receives a hand-sketched architectural blueprint 3 weeks prior, two live fittings, and a sealed archival vial of their custom botanical mix.

Philosophy
Symmetry born from asymmetry. Old-world motifs, modern negative space.
Material
Triple-sifted Rajasthani henna • French lavender • Ceylon oil
Promise
Named, numbered & never duplicated. Your pattern is yours forever.
